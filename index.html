Latino Student Union's Fundraising Art Show

What is the LSU fundraising Art Show?

The LSU fundraising art show is a one day event full of fun and exposure to latino culture with music and art. We will be showcasing student art to fundraise for the Amazon Rainforest as well as for our club. If you are interested in attending the art show click the RSVP button at the top so we can accomodate you best.  Join us on November 16, 2019 for an amazing day!

Amazon Rainforest Fundraising

We are raising money to help contribute in the preservation of the Amazon Rainforest. The Amazon Rainforest provides the earth with 20% of our oxygen and recently 7,200 acres burned. Our goal is to donate at least a thousand dollars.

Venue and Art Exhibits

The Art Show will be held in the new student center (501 E 10th st, Greenville, NC). Our main goal is to showcase art that represents hispanic heritage, and create more exposure to latin culture.
